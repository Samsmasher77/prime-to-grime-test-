Privacy Policy

Effective: April 20, 2026

Grime to Prime BBQ Cleaning ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit grimetoprime.space or contact us to book a grill cleaning, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Information We Collect

  • Contact information you provide: name, phone number, email address, and service address when you request a booking, quote, or information.
  • Service details: grill type, photos you share, preferred date, and notes relevant to the cleaning.
  • Automatic website data: IP address, browser, device type, pages visited, and referring site, collected through standard analytics tools.

How We Use Your Information

  • Respond to inquiries and schedule, provide, and complete grill cleaning services.
  • Send service-related messages (appointment confirmations, reminders, follow-ups).
  • Improve our website, marketing, and service quality.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share only with:

  • Service providers that help us operate the business (e.g., website hosting, analytics, email, scheduling tools) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Authorities when required by law.

Cookies and Analytics

Our site uses cookies and similar technologies — including Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics, and Meta (Facebook) Pixel — to understand site traffic and measure marketing performance. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Data Retention

We keep customer records as long as needed to provide services, maintain accurate business records, and meet legal requirements. You can request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or request a copy of the personal information we hold about you, and to opt out of marketing communications. California residents have specific rights under the CCPA. To exercise any of these rights, contact us using the details below.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
