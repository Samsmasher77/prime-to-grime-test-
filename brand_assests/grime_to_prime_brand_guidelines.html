06 — Do's & don'ts
Usage rules
Do
  • ✓ Use dark backgrounds — the brand lives in charcoal and black
  • ✓ Use Oswald for all headlines and buttons
  • ✓ BBQ Red for your main call-to-action only
  • ✓ Keep copy short, punchy, and confident
  • ✓ Use Flame Orange to draw the eye to secondary elements
Don't
  • ✗ Use a white or light background — kills the brand feel
  • ✗ Use more than 2 colors in a single button or element
  • ✗ Use thin or light-weight fonts — brand is bold
  • ✗ Use clip-art style BBQ imagery — keep photos dark & dramatic
  • ✗ Crowd the page — whitespace (dark space) is your friend
